# MAM Manager

Home Assistant integration for [MyAnonamouse](https://www.myanonamouse.net) (MAM).

- **Dashboard**: View your MAM user info (class, ratio, uploaded, downloaded, seedbonus, wedges, notifications) and whether you've donated today.
- **Daily options**: Automatically buy credit once per day and/or donate to the vault once per day (API URLs configured in options when provided).

Requires your MAM base URL, user ID, and session cookie (`mam_id`) from your browser after logging in.
